Business Partnership Relationship

TASoft LLC offers business partnership based plan to provide stock forecasting data products. Business partnership with TASoft LLC is formed on mutual benefits. There is no liability on either party. And this business partnership can be terminated by either party without any reason.

Benefits:
  • Customized Top Picks for 2.5 Month forecasting (limited 10 picks at the moment)
    • Customized request by average daily trading volume threshold.
    • Customized Reversal Picks or Uptrend picks or mixed.
    • Monthly top picks feed to partners (biweekly in the future).
  • Client requested stock forecasting (Limited to 5 picks per month)
    • Submit stock picks prior day, next day forecast delivery.
Agreements:
  • Partnership at will for both parties.
    • No liability for both parties.
    • Partnership can be terminated by both parties without reason.
  • Monthly trading results financial disclosure at will.
    • No payments unless profits beyond partner's regular profits.
    • Small percent profit sharing with TASoft LLC monthly beyond partner's regular profits.
